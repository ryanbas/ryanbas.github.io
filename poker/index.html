Cash Game Poker Payouts

This little app will calculate profit & loss for each player in your poker cash game.

Start by filling in the Buy-In Amount for the game and how many chips each Buy-In will give players.

Add players at the bottom until everybody in your cash game is in the table. Players can be removed by clicking the icon. Players are saved for the next time you visit this page but the chip counts reset.

At the end of your game fill in how many Buy-Ins each player paid for and their final chip count and click "Calculate P&L" to calculate the profit & loss for each player.
